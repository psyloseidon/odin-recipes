Berta's super deep fried oil

Berta got this recipe by accidently adding too much oil in her previously heated oil

She ended up deep frying the oil by mistake. According to her, the end result made her fries 35% crispier

You will need:

  • 200ml oil
  • 200ml oil but later

Before frying anything, fry your oil first!

  1. Heat up your oil on high heat
  2. Once it's hot, add the remaining oil
  3. Once that's hot, add your soon to be super deep fried whatever
  4. Crunch away
